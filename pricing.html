Why is LegalPack AI so much cheaper than a solicitor?

A solicitor review of a legal pack involves a qualified professional reading every document, dictating notes, drafting a report, and billing their time at £200–£400 per hour. For a typical 100–200 page pack, this takes several hours — hence the £429+VAT average cost.

LegalPack AI is a software tool that has been trained on thousands of UK auction legal packs. Once trained, it can read and analyse a pack in 3–4 minutes with no marginal cost per additional pack. The infrastructure cost per analysis is a fraction of the analysis fee — which is why we can price it at £9.99.

⚠️ Important

LegalPack AI is a due diligence tool, not a substitute for legal advice. For high-value purchases or complex packs, you should still instruct a solicitor before exchanging. Use LegalPack AI to decide which packs are worth paying for a full solicitor review — and to walk into that review already knowing the key issues.
