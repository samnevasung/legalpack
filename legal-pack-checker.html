What is a legal pack checker?

A legal pack checker is a tool that reads the documents in a UK property auction legal pack — the title register, lease, searches, special conditions, replies to enquiries, and management pack — and identifies risks, costs, and gaps that a buyer needs to know about before bidding.

LegalPack AI is an AI-powered legal pack checker built specifically for UK property auctions. It doesn't just scan for keywords — it reads documents the way a cautious UK solicitor would, understanding the context of what each clause means, what each register entry implies, and what should be in the pack but isn't. The result is a structured report with exact source references, cost estimates, and a financial exposure summary.

What LegalPack AI checks for

✓Flying freeholds and structural boundary issues
✓Short leases and marriage value exposure below 80 years
✓Chancel repair liability in Local Authority Search results
✓Unadopted roads and highway maintenance liability
✓Buyer's premiums and hidden auction administration fees
✓Service charge and ground rent arrears via LPE1 form
✓Restrictive covenants in the Charges Register
✓Absentee freeholder risk and building insurance status
✓Possessory title and other low-grade title classes
✓Special conditions of sale — 14-day completion, seller's costs, VAT
✓Missing searches, missing management pack, missing lease
✓Financial exposure summary with cost estimates for each risk

Every flag in the report includes the exact document name, page number, and clause or entry reference. You can verify any finding yourself — or share the report with your solicitor as a pre-structured summary of the key issues.
