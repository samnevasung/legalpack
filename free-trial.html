What can LegalPack AI find that you might miss?

A typical auction legal pack contains 80–300 pages written by the seller's solicitor. The issues that matter most — the ones that cost buyers tens of thousands of pounds — are rarely on the first page. Here are the things LegalPack AI flags that most buyers miss:

  • Hidden seller legal fees — often £1,500–£10,000, buried in Special Conditions, binding on completion
  • Short lease — under 80 years means mortgage difficulties; under 70 years typically means cash buyers only, with extension costs of £15–25k
  • No title guarantee — a lender sale with no title warranty, giving the buyer zero legal recourse for undisclosed title defects
  • Unadopted roads — the CON29 search reveals the access road is privately maintained, meaning the buyer inherits repair liability
  • Overage clauses — the seller retains a share of future planning uplift for 10–25 years after sale
  • Restrictive covenants — planning-era covenants that block any development or extension without consent
  • Missing building regulations — extensions or conversions with no completion certificate, requiring indemnity insurance
