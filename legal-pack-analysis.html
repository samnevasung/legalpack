What is a legal pack analysis?

A legal pack analysis is a structured review of all the documents in a UK property auction legal pack — the bundle of legal documents released by the seller's solicitor before the auction. It tells you what you're legally committing to if you win the lot.

The analysis focuses on the information that matters to a buyer before bidding: what the pack costs (including costs buried in special conditions), what title risks exist, whether the property is mortgageable, and what's missing from the pack that should be there. LegalPack AI delivers this in 3–4 minutes for any pack up to 300+ pages.

What LegalPack AI checks in every analysis

📋
Title issues
  • Class of title — absolute, good leasehold, possessory, or qualified
  • Flying freeholds and structural boundary issues
  • Restrictions in the B Register requiring third-party consent
  • Charges and unresolved mortgages in the C Register
  • Restrictive covenants and their enforceability
  • Cautions, bankruptcy restrictions, and court entries
🏠
Lease issues (leasehold properties)
  • Unexpired lease term and proximity to the 80-year marriage value threshold
  • Ground rent amount, escalation provisions, and Leasehold Reform Act 2022 compliance
  • Service charge structure, arrears, and LPE1 disclosures
  • Freeholder identity and contact details (absentee freeholder flags)
  • Section 20 major works notices and pending bills
  • Lease covenants restricting subletting, alterations, or use
⚠️
Special conditions of sale
  • Buyer's premium and administration fees — exact amounts with source references
  • Seller's legal costs payable by buyer — exact amounts
  • Search fee reimbursement requirements
  • Shortened completion periods (below 20 working days)
  • Limited or no title guarantee clauses
  • VAT on purchase price
  • Overage and clawback provisions
🔍
Search results
  • Chancel repair liability (Local Authority Search, Con29 section 3.7)
  • Unadopted roads and highway adoption status (Con29 section 2)
  • Planning enforcement notices and outstanding applications
  • Environmental risk flags (contaminated land, flood, ground stability)
  • Search age — lenders reject searches over 6 months old
📂
Document gaps
  • Missing Local Authority Search, drainage search, or environmental search
  • Missing lease on a leasehold property
  • Missing management pack / LPE1 form
  • Missing building regulations certificates
  • Missing planning permissions for extensions or conversions
  • References to documents in the pack that have not been provided
💷
Financial exposures
  • Total confirmed buyer additions above the hammer price
  • Estimated indemnity insurance costs for identified risks
  • Lease extension cost estimate (if lease is short)
  • Service charge and ground rent arrears (if inherited via special conditions)
  • Mortgageability assessment — flags that would cause lender refusal

Frequently asked questions

What does a legal pack analysis include?

A full legal pack analysis covers: title register (ownership, charges, restrictions, class of title); lease documents (for leasehold); special conditions (hidden buyer costs, shortened completion); search results (chancel repair, unadopted roads, planning); management pack / LPE1 (service charge arrears, building insurance); and document gaps. LegalPack AI covers all of these with cost estimates for every risk identified.

How long does a legal pack analysis take?

LegalPack AI delivers a full analysis in 3–4 minutes for most packs, including multi-document ZIPs of up to 300+ pages. Available 24/7 — including the night before your auction.

Is AI legal pack analysis accurate?

LegalPack AI is trained specifically on UK property auction legal packs, the Common Auction Conditions, HM Land Registry title register format, and UK leasehold legislation. Every flag includes the exact document, page, and clause reference so you can verify each finding yourself.

Can I use LegalPack AI instead of a solicitor?
